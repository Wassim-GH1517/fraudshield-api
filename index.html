BNA Banking Rules & Validation Logic
These are the real-world transaction rules enforced by the Transaction Validator before any fraud analysis. A transaction must pass ALL applicable rules before it is accepted and assigned a tracking ID.

💸 ATM Withdrawal Limits

Maximum 500 DT per ATM transaction (BNA standard limit)
Maximum 1,500 DT per day via ATM (tracked per customer CIN)
ATM only supports Withdrawal — not Transfer, Bill Payment, or Debit
Minimum withdrawal: 10 DT

💰 Balance Sufficiency Rules

Withdrawal, Debit, Transfer, Bill Payment — account balance must cover the amount
Minimum account balance after transaction: 0 DT (no overdraft)

📱 Device / Operation Compatibility

ATM: Withdrawal only
POS: Debit, Bill Payment (no transfer, no withdrawal)
Mobile / Desktop: Transfer, Bill Payment, Debit

⌚ Transfer Time Windows

Wire Transfers via Desktop/Mobile: available 24/7 — all days, all hours
ATM & POS: available 24 hours / 7 days
Bill Payments: available 24/7 (automated system)

📊 Transaction Limits by Account Type

Checking: max 5,000 DT per transfer
Savings: max 10,000 DT per transfer
Business: max 50,000 DT per transfer
Minimum transaction: 1 DT

✅ Other Validity Rules

Amount must be a positive number
Account balance must be non-negative
Amount cannot exceed account type maximum
Savings accounts cannot make outbound Transfers
